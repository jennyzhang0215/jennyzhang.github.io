About Me

I obtained Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020. My Ph.D supervisor was Prof. Irwin King. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015 under the supervision of Prof. Kenny Q. Zhu and Prof. Shi-Lin Wang. In 2020, I started my job as an applied scientist in Tencent.

I am especially interested in applying machine learning to real-world problems.
